About Me

I am currently a PhD candidate hydrologist at the Technical University of Munich and the German Aerospace Center (DLR). Interested in flood risk and socio-hydrology in a warming world and in the Anthropocene. I use earth observation data, such as satellite imagery, climate data, and socio-economic data, to assess flood impacts. My work particularly focuses on inequalities in flood and climate risk, as well as climate justice in the Anthropocene.
